X-99 Organization Issues
  
Goals
To get leveled racing.
To be able to provide sailing at all skill levels
To attract large fleets
To have a sailing class with regional, country and European level races.
To get low cost sailing and have possibility for owner-driven sailing
To get a high knowledge of the rules in helping us to avoid problems on the course and long nights in protest negotiations.
To foster gentleman's performance on and of the shore.

To be easy and take short time for a new X-99 sailor in getting the boat up to speed and be competitive
      To get a good relation to X-Yacht in effort to get good prices on boats and equipment and by that attracting more racers.  
      To help X-99ér to lay together in the harbors of the World.  
To build a X-99 knowledge bank
