There are many ways you can help Save the Children to continue its vital work in both the UK and around the world.

This page is designed to help you to decide which way of giving is best for you, with links to provide you with information on the variety of different types of gift you can make. The menu to the right offers a quick reference list.

One of the best ways to support our work is by making a regular donation of £2 a month or more (this is a form which opens in a new browser window - please close this once you have printed off the form to return the site).

Alternatively, you could help by making a donation now, either, online or by post (this is a form which opens in a new browser window - please close this once you have printed off the form to return the site).

To find out how you can help Save the Children through your workplace, please look at the corporate pages and the payroll giving pages.

You can help us while you shop, by taking out a Save the Children Visa Card. All new cards guarantee an immediate donation from the Co-operative Bank, and then every time a card is used it raises money for our education work world wide. You can also help Save the Children as you save by opening a savings account. The more you save the more the bank gives to Save the Children.

The legacy pages will show you how to include the people and causes you care for in your Will and you can also request a free, easy to understand guide to making and changing your Will.

Buy from Save the Children's gift catalogue and all the profits go to children.
