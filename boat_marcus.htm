Sail Racing Project, X-99. Sweden-344, Onyx, Team Bold Printing Group
Marcus comments about the X-99
        Very good  
          Very fast in all wind conditions.  
      Very good locking.  
          Second hand market. A big cruising fleet who wants to buy old racing sails. Good for both racers and cruiser.    
          Possible to (legal) trail after a car.    
      Possible to sleep on board under races. No costs for hotel.    
          Inboard engine. No wind, no problem to reach the starting line in time.    
      Toilet. No problems for girls doing #1 and for everyone if you have to do #2.    
          No problem to sail the boat all by your self.    
          Center lift. Easy to get up and down in the water.    
          Total comfort kit. Fresh water, sink, oven, heating system, stereo, table.....    
      Fractional rig with runners & check stay. Easy to shape the sails to what you like.    
          One design racing. Equal sailing.    
          Local and international competing.    
      Possible for ocean sailing.    
          Big crew. Everyone has something to do, fun to be a couple, more people to cheer cost work and fun sailing.    
         
          Not so good    
         

Boat weight.

Varies on different boats.

    
      Check stay. Should be on the runners instead of on the Genoa tracks.    
      Ruder. Little bit to small, staling quite easy.    
      Class rule.    
      Big crew. Hard to find an get everyone to work for the same goal.
