Jonas02-07-15

World Championship in Holland 2002: "Jonas! Where is the starting line? Are we clear ?" You got to play the oscillating shift. Forget about the persistent shifts. Gybing in heavy wind is like avoiding the falling bars in the computer game "Tetris".
We have found Eva Brauns sister Leif Braun. She was serving breakfast at the Regatta restaurant in Medemblik. Jesper is a hell of a runner. Jonas showed a dangerous work-out. "La Grotta" had the biggest pizzas and that was what we were looking for. Team Bold dancing at the Disco (alone without camera, thanks).
Danne, our timing man, who once drank twenty four Red Bull vodka, found a new way of drinking big beers by timing with his new Musto starting watch. Don´t try to beat him, you could die ! All Danne speakes about after just one beer is, "I can´t wait! I want to go out sailracing !" He can show you how to trim the Spinnaker and speed away from the Nescafé team.
Except for the terms "starboard" and "port" you have to know what "a Håkan shift" is. "KPMG" didn´t know that term and lost 15 places. In Holland you can´t buy things for money, first you have to buy a ticket. Håkan, the nice guy who mist the boat and got a bath, when he showed the boat to a Dutch opponent. Special thanks to Chrysler for the transportation and Scotch for the bathroom song in Delirious Mind.
