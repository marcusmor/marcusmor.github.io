The X99 - The Most Successful 33ft One Design Ever

15 years after her birth the X-99 has established strong classes in Denmark, North and South Germany, England, Switzerland, Holland, Ireland, France, Sweden and Hong Kong. Now the largest 33' European Class. The X-99 offers you more than any other 33' yacht - great class and handicap racing plus cruising comfort and plenty of pure sailing pleasure.
