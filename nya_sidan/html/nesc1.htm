Sail Racing Project, X-99. Sweden-344, Onyx, Team Bold Printing Group
    
Team Nescafé, Danmark

This team has won the Worlds the four last times. Everyone wants to beat them. I hope they continue to the worlds 2004, so that we get the privilege to beat them.
A heavy crew is one of the most important things for good results, but there is a classrule where the maximum crew-weight is 512kg. We did everything we could to lose weight the last month with little food and long walks. The last ten days we lost 4kg each. Just before the final weight test we were jogging for one hour in long pants and shirt. We made our official weigh test at the race office at the same time as team Nescafé. They come jogging to the weigh-in in complete sailing-outfit as above, - that´s World Class weightlosing.
