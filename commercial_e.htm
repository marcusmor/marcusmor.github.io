Rent the boat with Skipper.

Sail in the wonderful Swedish archipelago.

Rent the boat with skipper for all kinds of activities from one hour up to couple of days. More boats can be arranged and takes up to eight persons. We have good possibilities to put together a package that will please all kinds of interests. Anything from passive sailing where you just enjoy being onboard to active Match-racing with two boats.

The skipper is very skilled. Individuals who are a somewath afraid of the sea and sailing, have big possibilities to feel secured during the activities.
